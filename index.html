Harry Potter — The Complete Series

Seven novels by J.K. Rowling. Tap a book to read a short summary — no magic (or JavaScript) required.

Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone J.K. Rowling · Book 1 Show summary

Eleven-year-old Harry discovers he is a wizard and leaves the Dursleys for Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He makes friends, learns the truth about his parents’ death, and faces Lord Voldemort for the first time as the Dark Lord seeks the Sorcerer’s Stone.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets J.K. Rowling · Book 2 Show summary

In his second year, Harry hears whispers of the Chamber of Secrets and attacks on Muggle-born students. With Ron and Hermione, he uncovers a fifty-year-old mystery involving a memory of Tom Riddle and a deadly basilisk loose beneath the castle.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban J.K. Rowling · Book 3 Show summary

Sirius Black escapes Azkaban and is believed to be hunting Harry. Dementors guard Hogwarts while Harry learns about his parents’ friends—and that truth, loyalty, and time can change everything you thought you knew.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire J.K. Rowling · Book 4 Show summary

The Triwizard Tournament brings two other schools to Hogwarts—and somehow Harry is forced to compete. Dangerous tasks lead to a graveyard confrontation where Voldemort rises again, marking a turning point in the wizarding war.

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix J.K. Rowling · Book 5 Show summary

The Ministry denies Voldemort’s return; Dolores Umbridge tightens control at Hogwarts. Harry wrestles with visions and anger while Dumbledore’s Army prepares to fight—and a prophecy is revealed that binds Harry and Voldemort forever.

Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince J.K. Rowling · Book 6 Show summary

Dumbledore gives Harry private lessons into Voldemort’s past and Horcruxes. Draco Malfoy’s mission darkens the year, and Harry inherits a potions book annotated by the mysterious “Half-Blood Prince”—leading to choices that change Hogwarts forever.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows J.K. Rowling · Book 7 Show summary

Harry, Ron, and Hermione hunt Horcruxes on the run as Voldemort’s regime takes hold. Myths of the Deathly Hallows converge with the final battle at Hogwarts—where sacrifice, love, and courage decide the fate of the wizarding world.
